## 💡 TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Midjourney v6.1** gives the most photorealistic results for fashion
2. Add `--sref [URL]` in Midjourney to maintain visual consistency across shots
3. For DALL-E 3: Start with "A professional fashion photograph of..."
4. Use **Upscale** in Midjourney (U1-U4) for print-quality outputs
5. Run each prompt 3–4 times and select the best variation
6. To get consistent faces across shots, use **--cref** in Midjourney v6.1
